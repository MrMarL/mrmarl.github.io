FAQ

How to start?(admin)

You write "/ob set", where the first island will be located. To create a new island "/ob join". (If the island already exists for the player, then he will get to it) Each new island +100 blocks on the X coordinate.

How to start?

"/ob join" or "/ob j".

How to invite a friend to the island?

You write "/ob invite 'playername'". Your friend writes "/ob accept".

How to leave the island?

You write "/ob IDreset" and your data about the island is completely erased. (Does not affect other players on this island) After that, you can create a new island for yourself "/ob join".
